Introducing Presently

Here are a few reasons why you should use Presently for your next birthday!

Empowering for Children

Empower children to think thoughtfully about what they need, communicate their decisions, learn the value of saving, and pass it forward with tzedakah or charity

Convenient for Parents

Spend less time returning unwanted or duplicate gifts, have more control over what their children receive, and present meaningful lessons to children around gifts, saving, and giving back

Sustainable Gifting

Gifts have a longer life-spans, heaps of plastic, packaging, and gift wrap waste are being eliminated, and children learn to take care of our community and planet - in the present and the future

Lasting Memories

Children can cherish their gifts longer and parents can spend less time gift buying and more time making memories celebrating their kids.
