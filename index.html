Here are a few reasons why you should use Presently for your next birthday!

Empowering for children

Empowered to think thoughtful about and cherish what they need, save up for college or learn the value of tzedaka

Less time spent on returning unwanted or duplicate gifts, more control over what their kids receive, and more meaningful lessons to kids around gifts, saving, and giving back

Sustainable gifting

Greater lifespan of toys, less plastic purchased, no more heaps of packaging and gift wrap waste, and teaching kids how to take care of our community and planet - in the present and the future

Lasting memories

More cherished presents = more cherished memories!
